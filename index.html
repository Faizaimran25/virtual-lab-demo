Kaiju No 8

Kaiju No. 8 is a Japanese manga series by Naoya Matsumoto, set in a world where monstrous Kaiju attack Japan. The story follows Kafka Hibino, a man who gains the ability to transform into a Kaiju himself, leading to a struggle between his humanity and newfound powers.

Dragon Ball Daima

Dragon Ball Daima is a new anime series by Akira Toriyama, set in the Dragon Ball universe. It features Goku and his friends mysteriously transformed into child-like versions of themselves as they embark on a new adventure to uncover the truth behind this phenomenon.

Solo Leveling

Solo Leveling is a South Korean web novel by Chugong, later adapted into a popular manhwa and anime. It follows Jinwoo Sung, a weak hunter who gains a unique ability to level up endlessly, transforming into the world's strongest hunter.
